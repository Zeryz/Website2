Pool Tournament Manager - Instructions

Before Starting

  • Type player names in the input field and press Enter to add them.
  • Remove players before starting by clicking the × on their tag.
  • Choose Single or Double Elimination from the dropdown.
  • Click "Start Tournament" when ready (at least 2 players required).

During the Tournament

  • Click a player's name in a match to declare them the winner.
  • The winner is highlighted in green.
  • Once all matches in a round are complete, click "Generate Next Round".
  • Add late players anytime using the "Add Late Player" button.
  • Remove players from standings if needed (with confirmation).
  • Progress is automatically saved in your browser.

End of Tournament

  • When down to 2 players, the final match determines 1st and 2nd.
  • A 3rd Place Match is automatically created between the two semi-final losers.
  • Final placements (1st-4th) are shown on the podium.

Tips

  • Pairings are random but avoid rematches when possible.
  • Byes are balanced fairly.
  • Use "Load Saved" to resume a previous tournament.
